  • Punch at the generic political class and the system — never a real person, community, religion, caste, or region.
  • Always watermark or caption clearly as satire / parody. Honesty is the whole brand.

  • Keep it funny, keep it kind, keep the voter the hero. End on the tagline: "This is a joke. Which is more than the others can say."

The Universal Prompt Recipe

Stuck on wording? Paste this skeleton into any image, video, or chat AI and fill the blanks. Consistency is what makes a meme army look like a movement.

STYLE: [vintage Bollywood poster / agitprop / Pixar 3D / folk art / photoreal]
SUBJECT: a heroic, friendly cockroach mascot ("Roachu") with a purple-inked finger
PALETTE: chitin black-brown, election-ink violet, gold, aged-paper cream
MOOD: deadpan-sincere, warm, satirical — never cruel
TEXT/CAPTION: [your slogan] + small print: "This is a joke. Which is more than the others can say."
AVOID: any real person, party, community, religion, or region
