Make it un-ignorable

The receipts

RoachMap publishes claims with evidence, never accusations. Here's exactly what's real, what's yours, and where every number comes from.

What the headline number is

The big percentage is the official overall unemployment rate for your state (Current Weekly Status, persons 15+), as reported by the Government of India's Periodic Labour Force Survey — not a number we made up, and not your grievance count.

The national figures

  • Graduate unemployment ~13% and "1 in 3 in their early 20s" — PLFS / Data For India analysis, Nov 2025.
  • Youth (15–29) unemployment ~14.9%, November 2025 monthly PLFS bulletin.
  • 10M+ new workforce entrants a year — MoSPI / PLFS population estimates.

Sources

  • Economic Survey 2025–26, Statistical Appendix Table 8.12 — state-wise UR (CWS).
  • Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS), Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation.
  • Data For India — "What do India's graduates do?", Nov 2025.
  • Accessed: May 2026.

Your pin is unverified — on purpose

Anything you typed is stored only in this browser tab and is shown as "Unverified." In the full RoachMap it would only gain weight once independent users corroborate it and an official notification, RTI reply, or court order is attached and hash-checked. A "broken promise" needs a dated, on-the-record source or it can't be pinned at all. No source = no pin.

Why this exists

A million angry comments are noise an official can ignore. A district map of sourced, time-stamped reports is a story a journalist runs and a court accepts. The point is to convert vibes into evidence — and to track broken promises from every party in power, everywhere. The day it maps only one side, it dies as evidence.

Honest limits

This is an awareness build — a concept of the spec, not the live verified national database. It shows real official statistics and lets you draft a pin; it does not claim your individual report is confirmed. Real grievance verification, anti-brigading, and legal review come before any national launch.

Part of the CJP awareness toolkit

RoachMap is one of three companion pieces. The Playbook carries the movement's identity and doctrine; the Content Pack is its meme-army field kit. Both are openly satire / parody. RoachMap is the opposite number: the neutral, evidence-based layer that turns the energy the satire generates into sourced, verifiable grievance. The two are kept deliberately separate — the map never editorialises, and the commentary never sits on the map. Open the Playbook → · Open the Content Pack →

This is product/awareness guidance, not legal advice.
